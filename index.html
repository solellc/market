About Us

With God's leading, Sole has brought beauty to the Shoals for years. Originally expressed through dance, we are now offering the community beauty through flower bouquets. We affectionately call them our “bundles”. Jay has a background in technology and is actively involved in growing the local tech community. Leslie carries a passion for movement and continues to dance through her worship wherever she is. Liam and Cyprus are full of life, energy, and learning more about who they are every day. Together we are excited to share a new piece of our heart to our home-sweet-home, the Shoals.

Whether it’s flowers for your sweetie, a celebration, or a thoughtful gift, we would love to wrap a bundle for you. Enjoy the samples below of our bundles. We look forward to bringing beauty, joy, and fragrance to your life!
